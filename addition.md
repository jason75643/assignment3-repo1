If you have two positive integers like 10 and 7, and we are adding them
10+7
Just add them together
10+7=17
If there is a negative, then you subtract it 
-10+7 or 10+(-7)
The first one, a negative plus a positive, you will add 7 from a negative 10
However, for the second one, a positive minus a number, then u just subtract
-10+7=-3
10+(-7)=3
